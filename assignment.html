I'm a big fan of dogs. They are truly man's best friend! They will love you unconditionally. My dog is a Labradoodle. Labradoodles were bred to be hypo-allergenic, as well as have the best traits of a Lab as well as a Poodle. My dog is the best! She is loving, loyal, and a total sweetheart. Here is a quote from Wikipedia about Labradoodles:

A Labradoodle (/ˈlæbrəduːdəl/) is a crossbreed dog created by crossing the Labrador Retriever and the Standard, Miniature, or Toy poodle. The term first appeared in 1955, but was not initially popular. Labradoodles are considered a good choice for people with canine dander allergies, since some have the same hypoallergenic coat as their poodle ancestors
